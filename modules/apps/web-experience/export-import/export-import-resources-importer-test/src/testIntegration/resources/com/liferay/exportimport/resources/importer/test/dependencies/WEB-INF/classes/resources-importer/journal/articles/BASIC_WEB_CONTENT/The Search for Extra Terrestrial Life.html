The Search for Extra Terrestrial Intelligence may be a good thing, or it may be a bad thing. On the one hand, if there's more life out there, wouldn't we want to get in touch with it? On the other hand, if you've seen enough sci-fi movies, you'll know that alien life might not be friendly.

The best case scenario would be how Star Trek presents first contact. Other benevolent races contact earth and seek to share technology with us, once we've reached a certain level ourselves.

The worst case scenario would be Independence Day. Extremely advanced and very unfriendly aliens make contact and seek to steal our resources and subjugate us.

Either way, if someone's out there looking for us, they will eventually find us--in addition to various unmanned vessels that we've launched, a century's worth of radio and television broadcasts have leaked out and are traveling out into the far reaches of space. Somewhere out there, a Martian is complaining that he's already seen these Seinfeld reruns.
